refund policy

Eligibility for Refunds

Refunds are available within 30 days of your purchase. Items must be unused, in their original condition, with tags attached.
Proof of purchase (receipt or order confirmation) is required for all refunds.

Non-Refundable Items

Gift cards and sale items are non-refundable. Custom or personalized products are non-refundable unless there is a manufacturing defect.

How to Request a Refund

To initiate a refund, contact our customer service team at [customer support email] with your order details.
Once your return is received and inspected, we will notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund.

Refund Process

Approved refunds will be processed within 7-10 business days. Refunds will be credited to your original payment method.

Shipping Costs

Shipping costs are non-refundable. If you receive a refund,
the cost of return shipping will be deducted unless the return is due to an error on our part.
