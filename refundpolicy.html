Shipping Policy

Processing Times

Orders are processed within 1-3 business days, excluding weekends and holidays.
You will receive a confirmation email with tracking information once your order has shipped.

Shipping Options and Delivery Times

Standard Shipping: 5-7 business days.
Expedited Shipping: 2-3 business days.
Overnight Shipping: 1 business day.
Delivery times may vary based on your location and the shipping option selected.

Shipping Costs

Shipping costs are calculated at checkout based on the destination and shipping method.
Free standard shipping is available for orders over $75 (before taxes).

International customers are responsible for customs duties, taxes, and any additional fees.
