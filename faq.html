Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is Routeboy?

Routeboy is a clothing brand focused on empowering individuals in their journey through life.
We design apparel that blends style with functionality, supporting you as you tackle your daily goals and challenges.

How do I place an order?

To place an order, browse our product pages, select your desired items and sizes, and add them to your cart.
Once you’re ready, go to the cart and follow the prompts to complete your checkout process.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), as well as PayPal. All transactions are secure and encrypted for your protection.

How can I track my order?

Once your order is shipped, we’ll send a confirmation email with a tracking number. You can use this number to track your order on the carrier's website.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy on all unused items. If you’re not satisfied with your purchase, contact our customer service to initiate a return. For more details, please refer to our Refund Policy.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we ship worldwide! Shipping fees and delivery times vary by destination and are calculated at checkout.

How can I contact customer support?

You can reach us through our Contact Us page or email us at support@routeboy.com. Our team is available Monday through Friday from 9 AM to 6 PM EST.

How can I stay updated on new releases?

Join our newsletter to stay informed about new drops, exclusive offers, and other updates! You can subscribe here.

Do you have a size guide?

Yes, we offer a size guide on each product page to help you find the perfect fit. For additional assistance, feel free to contact our support team.

Can I change or cancel my order?

If you need to make changes or cancel an order, please contact us as soon as possible. Once an order is processed, we may not be able to make changes.
